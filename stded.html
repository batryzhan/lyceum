Educational Program

The lyceum provides deep knowledge in the fields of natural sciences, mathematics, and engineering.

Educational Process

The educational process at the lyceum is organized based on the state compulsory educational program approved by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

"Bilim Innovation" Foundation's Plan

The educational plan developed by the "Bilim Innovation" Foundation is approved by the Head of the Institution and implemented at the lyceum. This plan ensures that students receive a quality education.

Language of Instruction

The lyceum offers instruction in both the state language and English. This helps develop language skills and provides opportunities for students to receive education at an international level.

Value-Based Education

This program implements measures to instill values within the lyceum and integrate them into the educational process.

Educational Process

The educational process at the lyceum is organized based on the state compulsory educational program approved by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Formation of the Organizational Group

Each academic year, values to be promoted within the program are selected, with each value emphasized for at least one month. The selection considers the opinions of all staff, students, and parents.

Integrating Values into the Learning Process

The organizational group incorporates values into educational programs. Values are included in lesson plans, extracurricular activities, subject associations, group activities, and events with parents.
