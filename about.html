History and Structure of the Lyceum

The Lyceum — a source of knowledge and education.

Opening and Purpose of the Lyceum

Regional "Education-Innovation" boarding lyceum for talented children No. 9 – a prestigious educational institution that provides knowledge and education to the talented youth of the Syr region. The Lyceum was opened in 1993 as a Kazakh-Turkish boys' lyceum.

Educational Focus and Languages of Instruction

The Lyceum started working in the natural sciences and mathematics direction. Here, subjects such as mathematics, physics, biology, chemistry, and informatics are taught in English.

Achievements and Status

The Lyceum's talented students have consistently won prizes in national and international competitions. Through these achievements, the lyceum has become one of the top educational institutions in the region.
